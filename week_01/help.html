How to Use DoggoSnap ?

Follow these quick steps to explore breeds and view real images.

Don't know the dog breed name?

Browse our dropdown list of all dog breeds. You can scroll or type to find the breed you're curious about.

Select the breed you want to see.

Click on the breed name from the dropdown menu.

Click the “Submit” button.

This will fetch a random image of the selected breed from our database.

View the breed image.

Observe the dog's size, coat, and appearance to understand the breed better.

Repeat if needed.

Click submit again to see another random image of the same breed.

After you got the breed name and pictures.

You can now freely see how they look before you decide to buy or adopt a dog.
